NOVAT – Business Project Management Software

NOVAT (formerly Mooncrew) is a legitimate business productivity application for companies and teams. This is not a proxy, gambling, adult, or malicious site — it is professional web-based business software hosted over HTTPS.

Business purpose

NOVAT helps organizations manage work: tasks, Kanban boards, Eisenhower prioritization, shared calendars, working-time tracking (ArbZG-compliant), team chat, and AI-assisted day planning. Used for normal office and project work — category: Business / Web-based Applications.

Features for teams

  • Task and project management with boards and lists
  • Calendar, appointments and team scheduling
  • Time tracking and overtime overview
  • Team messaging and organization admin
  • Secure login (email / OAuth) via Supabase
